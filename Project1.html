Switzerland

A trip to Switzerland is a place that people of all ages can enjoy, but we have carefully selected activities that couples planning their honeymoon can enjoy. A country of nature that has been overcome and protected by indomitable will, a place where the whole can be called a tourist destination, blue, green, white, and red came to mind as the colors symbolizing Switzerland. The snowy mountains of the Alps, green meadows, mountain trains, and the Swiss flag contain images that reveal four colors, and the Gelica Adobe font with a friendly tone is used to convey a welcoming feeling, especially to those who are new to Switzerland.

Things to do in Switzerland

Nicknamed the 'Top of Europe', the Jungfrau is one of the most attractive tourist destinations in Europe. Another attraction that cannot be left out of Jungfrau is the mountain train. Due to the nature of Switzerland's mountainous terrain, railways were developed early on. The Jungfrau Railway is the highest station in Europe, which has been in operation for over 100 years, and is the pride of Swiss railways as it is registered as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. You can see the beautiful village on the meadow from the running train and experience the beautiful atmosphere of the Swiss mountains.

Murren, a very pretty fairy tale town settled in the high mountain range, is one of the most famous places in the Jungfrau region. Cute Swiss wooden buildings lined up, the Swiss flag raised everywhere, and walking through a very peaceful picturesque village, you will come across the Murren log, which is famous for taking photos to share on social media. The view of the endless sky and the cliff in the background is spectacular. Paragliding across the peaks is a must-do activity here.
